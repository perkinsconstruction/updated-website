Why Choose Perkins Construction?

Experience, Quality, and Client Focus Since 1984.

Embarking on a custom home build or significant renovation is a major undertaking, requiring a partner you can implicitly trust. At Perkins Construction, we understand that we're not just constructing walls and roofs; we're helping you realize a vision for your life. That's why our entire process is built on a foundation of clear communication, mutual respect, and shared goals.

For over four decades serving the Ann Arbor community, our growth hasn't been fueled by flashy advertising, but by the genuine satisfaction of our clients. We build relationships as carefully as we build homes, fostering a collaborative environment where your ideas are heard, your concerns are addressed promptly, and your expectations are consistently exceeded. This combination allows us to create spaces that are not only aesthetically beautiful and functionally perfect for your lifestyle but also efficient, durable, and built to last for generations. Choose Perkins Construction for a partnership defined by expertise, integrity, and a commitment to making your dream home a reality.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your process for starting a new project?

Our process typically begins with an initial consultation and site visit to discuss your vision, needs, and budget. We then move into design development (if needed), followed by detailed estimating, contract finalization, permitting, construction, and final walkthroughs. We prioritize clear communication at every stage.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary greatly depending on the scope and complexity. A simple bathroom remodel might take a few weeks, while a custom home build can take several months to over a year. We provide a projected schedule and start date once the scope is finalized.

Who will be working on my project?

We have a dedicated in-house team of exceptionally talented carpenters and project managers. We also work with trusted and reliable subcontractors that we have longstanding relationships with and who mirror our philosophies. Together, you can be assured that the people working on your project will demonstrate the highest levels of pride in their work and respect for your family and home.

Can you accommodate my work from home job?

We are always willing to accomodate your needs. Our workers and subcontractors are courteous and respectful. We will install temporary protections to help contain our work and will always notify you of any tasks that might be disruptive to your work and seek to minimize those disruptions.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes, Perkins Construction handles all necessary building permits and coordinates required inspections with local authorities as part of our comprehensive project management services.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Ann Arbor and the surrounding communities within Washtenaw County, including Saline, Dexter, Chelsea, and Ypsilanti. Please contact us to discuss your specific project location.
