A Legacy of Quality Craftsmanship

For over four decades, Perkins Construction has been synonymous with exceptional residential construction and remodeling in Ann Arbor. Founded in 1984, our enduring success is built not on advertising, but on the foundation of strong client relationships and consistent word-of-mouth referrals – a testament to the quality and integrity we bring to every project.

We understand that building or remodeling a home is a significant journey. Our experienced team partners with you every step of the way, combining time-honored building practices with innovative solutions to create spaces that are both beautiful and enduringly functional. We pride ourselves on meticulous attention to detail, transparent communication, and a commitment to exceeding expectations.
